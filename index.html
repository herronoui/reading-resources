Books Worth Reading

A short list of books for robotics, controls, and aerospace.

Robotics

  • Modern Robotics
    Lynch & Park
    Kinematics, dynamics, planning. Free online.
  • Introduction to Robotics
    Craig
    Classic intro. Good first robotics book.
  • Probabilistic Robotics
    Thrun, Burgard, Fox
    SLAM, localization, state estimation.
  • Planning Algorithms
    LaValle
    Motion planning. Free online.

Controls

  • Feedback Control of Dynamic Systems
    Franklin, Powell, Emami-Naeini
    The standard controls textbook.
  • Robot Modeling and Control
    Spong, Hutchinson, Vidyasagar
    Dynamics + control for manipulators.
  • Optimal Control and Estimation
    Stengel
    LQR, Kalman filtering, robust control.

Aerospace

  • Fundamentals of Astrodynamics
    Bate, Mueller, White
    Orbital mechanics. Short and dense.
  • Aircraft Control and Simulation
    Stevens, Lewis, Johnson
    Flight dynamics and autopilot design.

Math & Foundations

  • Linear Algebra Done Right
    Axler
    Clean linear algebra without determinants first.
  • Introduction to Linear Algebra
    Strang
    Intuitive. Great lectures on MIT OCW.
  • Convex Optimization
    Boyd & Vandenberghe
    Used everywhere in modern robotics. Free online.
